Hugo Migration Service Tiers #

We offer transparent, straightforward pricing with three service tiers designed for different business needs. All prices include everything needed for a successful migration and launch.

Pricing Questions & Examples #

How do you determine the final price within each tier? #

Pricing depends on:

  • Content volume - More pages take longer to migrate and optimize
  • Site complexity - Custom functionality costs more than standard pages
  • Design requirements - Basic theme vs. fully custom design
  • Integrations needed - APIs, forms, e-commerce, etc.
  • Your team’s technical level - More training needed = higher cost

Example: Professional Tier Breakdown #

Client: Growing Nonprofit (60 pages, needs custom design, wants CDN)

  • Content migration (60 pages): 12 hours @ $125/hr = $1,500
  • Theme customization & design: 20 hours @ $125/hr = $2,500
  • Content optimization & SEO: 8 hours @ $125/hr = $1,000
  • Hosting setup (GitHub + CloudFront + DNS): 6 hours @ $125/hr = $750
  • Training and launch support: 6 hours @ $125/hr = $750
  • Total: ~$6,500 (within Professional tier of $5,000-10,000)

What’s NOT included? #

We don’t include:

  • Content writing or rewriting (beyond formatting)
  • Photography or graphic design
  • Video production
  • Paid advertising setup
  • Sales page copywriting

We can recommend partners for these services, or we can provide estimates for incorporating them into your project.

Do you offer payment plans? #

Yes. For larger projects, we can arrange:

  • 50% upfront, 50% on completion
  • Phased payments tied to project milestones
  • Custom arrangements for long-term support contracts

What if the migration is more complex than expected? #

We provide a detailed assessment before starting, so surprises are rare. If additional complexity emerges during the project:

  • We’ll discuss it with you immediately
  • We’ll provide a revised estimate
  • We’ll only proceed with your approval
  • We never surprise you with unexpected charges

How do you handle scope creep? #

Our process includes:

  • Detailed upfront assessment and scope definition
  • Clear statement of what’s included
  • Regular communication about progress
  • Any out-of-scope requests get a separate estimate

This keeps costs predictable and prevents surprises.

ROI & Cost Savings #

Annual Savings Comparison #

WordPress Site (typical costs):

  • Hosting: $300/month = $3,600/year
  • Premium plugins: $500/year
  • Maintenance time: 10 hrs/month × $100 = $12,000/year
  • Total: $16,100/year

Hugo Site (typical costs):

  • Hosting: $50/month = $600/year
  • No plugins needed: $0/year
  • Maintenance time: 1 hr/month × $100 = $1,200/year
  • Total: $1,800/year

Annual savings: $14,300 ✨
